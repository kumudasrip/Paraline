System Tweaks

Configure performance options and system integration settings.

Performance Mode

Balance visual quality with system performance. Lower modes reduce GPU usage.

Framerate Target

Control the visualizer's update rate to balance animation smoothness with power efficiency.

Dynamic Coordinated Throttling (36–60 FPS) Automatically adapts rendering speed to the active theme's mechanical needs. Ensures a highly smooth visual baseline while keeping GPU load under 2% on most machines. Highly recommended for standard setups.
Battery Saver (Lock 30 FPS) Locks the visualizer loop to 30 FPS across all themes. Drastically reduces processor overhead and power consumption. Highly recommended if you are playing demanding games or running Paraline on a laptop on battery.
High Refresh (Unlocked / V-Sync Sync) Removes artificial caps, rendering at your monitor's native refresh rate (e.g. 75Hz, 144Hz, 240Hz). Delivers butter-smooth, ultra-responsive physics. Best for powerful desktop PCs with dedicated graphics cards and gaming monitors.
